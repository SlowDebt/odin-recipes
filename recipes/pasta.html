Pasta

  • 8 ounces mostaccioli pasta, or similar size pasta
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (4 ounce) can mushrooms, drained
  • 1 (28 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Bring a large pot of lightly salted boil water to a boil. Cook mostaccioli until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain well.

  2. Step 2

    Meanwhile, cook ground beef and onions in a skillet over medium heat until browned.

  3. Step 3

    Combine mushrooms, spaghetti sauce, shredded mozzarella cheese, pasta, and ground beef-onion mixture in a large bowl. Transfer to a greased 9x13-inch casserole dish.

  4. step 4

    Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) until very hot, about 20 minutes.
